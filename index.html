About the Doctor

Excellence in
Digestive Health

Dr. Dinesh Choudhary is a highly skilled Surgical Gastroenterologist based in Jodhpur, Rajasthan, bringing world-class gastrointestinal and laparoscopic care to Western India. Trained at the prestigious King George's Medical University (KGMU), Lucknow, he holds an MCh in Surgical Gastroenterology — one of the most rigorous specializations in medicine.

With over 12 years of experience and 5000+ successful procedures, Dr. Choudhary is known for his precision-driven surgical outcomes and deeply compassionate patient-first approach.
