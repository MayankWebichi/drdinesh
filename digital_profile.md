## 2. Patient Search Intent Analysis
Patients searching for a gastroenterologist or gastrosurgeon online typically fall into three search intent categories:

1. **Transactional (High Intent - Ready to Book):**
   * *What they search:* "best gastro surgeon in Jodhpur", "gastroenterologist doctor near me Shastri Nagar", "Dr Dinesh Choudhary clinic timing".
   * *Their need:* Looking for direct contact details, clinic address, timings, and a direct way to book an appointment immediately.
2. **Informational/Symptom-driven (Medium Intent - Researching):**
   * *What they search:* "gallbladder stone surgery cost in Jodhpur", "treatment for severe acidity/GERD", "who treats liver problems".
   * *Their need:* Informative, easy-to-read guides on symptoms, treatment options, and surgical procedures. They need reassurance before booking.
3. **Navigational/Verification (Trust building):**
   * *What they search:* "Dr Dinesh Choudhary reviews", "Dr Dinesh Choudhary Jodhpur rating", "is Dr Dinesh Choudhary a good doctor".
   * *Their need:* Patient testimonials, reviews, verified credentials (MBBS, MS, MCh), and success rates.

## 7. The Simple Conversion Funnel Flow

1. **Discovery (Traffic Source):** The user has a digestive issue and searches on Google. They see Dr. Dinesh Choudhary's clinic listed on Google Maps (with 4.9★ rating and 1500+ reviews) or via local search results.
2. **Engagement & Trust (Website Landing Page):** They click the website link. The website immediately displays his credentials (SMS Jaipur & KGMU Lucknow), list of treated conditions, and positive patient testimonials. This mitigates fear and builds instant trust.
3. **Conversion (CTA):** The visitor sees a highly visible "Book Appointment via WhatsApp" button. Since typing a message can be tedious, clicking the button instantly opens WhatsApp with a pre-filled template.
4. **Retention & Visit:** The clinic staff responds with available timings. The appointment is scheduled, and a confirmation with a Google Maps location link is sent back to the patient.
